Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I enroll in a course? ▶

Go to the Courses page, select the course you're interested in, and click the “Enroll” button. You’ll be guided through the process step by step.

2. Is there a fee for using SAHub? ▶

SAHub offers both free and premium plans. You can access basic content for free, while premium features require a subscription starting at $4.99/month.

3. Can I access the courses offline? ▶

Courses are currently only available online. Offline support is planned in future updates.

4. How do I update my profile information? ▶

Go to your Profile page and click on “Edit Profile” to update your details. Changes take effect immediately.

5. What if I forget my password? ▶

Click “Forgot Password” on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions to reset it.

6. Who can I contact for technical support? ▶

Visit our Contact page to reach our support team. We usually respond within 24 hours.
